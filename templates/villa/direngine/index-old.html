About

Narvik Villa Fasilities

Situated next to Krandangan National Park, you will be nestled in a beautiful valley surrounded by the lush forest. The villa is perfect for a big family or a group of friend. Only 5 minutes by a car or scooter and 20 minutes on a walk to the beach. Our 4-bedroom spacious villa offers an incredible nature view, a private pool, a BBQ grill, a fully equipped kitchen, an advanced Jura coffee machine, PlayStation 4, Netflix, karaoke, and free WIFI available in all areas. Breakfast is served for free. Lunch and dinner can be ordered outside by our staff or prepared by our professional chef at the villa (surcharge). Rent a car or scooter and a half or full day excursion can be organized by request.
